Why Blendy?

Because our shots are a tasty and efficient alternative to traditional tablets or capsules. They are:

Are better absorbed

The active ingredients in Blendy are already dissolved, which facilitates their absorption in the digestive tract.

Can be consumed with ease

There is no need to swallow tablets, which may cause difficulties to some of us.

Have higher concentration of active ingredients

They contain a larger amount of active ingredients, allowing for higher doses in one serving.

Can be taken anywhere… and they look splendid!

No need to drag a whole pharmacy with you. At work, on holidays, during travels, while driving… you can take a Blendy out and instantly provide your body with all the nutrient it needs. Convenient, isn’t it? And looks astonishing!

Lack of fillers

Unlike tablets and capsules, our shots contain fewer - or do not include at all - additional ingredients, such as coating or fillers. There is simply no need for them.

Act faster

Due to better absorption, the effects of Blendy are felt faster than those of pills, powder or tablets.

Tasty great!

Our shots come in a variety of flavors, which makes the consumption experience enjoyable and yummy.
